ABOUT US

VIN-KJ AUTO SERVICES

VIN-KJ AUTO SERVICES is a trusted name in the automotive aftermarket industry. We specialize in professional car window tinting, Paint Protection Film (PPF), car wrapping, and auto detailing. Our mission is simple: deliver quality products and services at fair prices, with customer satisfaction at the heart of everything we do.

Located on Lanet Road, off Baricho Road in Nairobi, we serve vehicle owners across the region. Whether you need a full tint job, PPF installation, a professional car wrap, or expert auto detailing — we're here to help.

With a team of experienced technicians and a commitment to using only high-quality materials, we ensure every customer drives away satisfied.
